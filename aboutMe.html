About Me

Nayeli Miranda

I am a visual artist from Austin, Texas. While searching for a new career path I encountered some information online about UX/UI Design, I decided to look more in depth into it and find out more about it. It caught my attention because it combines various disciplines such as design and technology, so I decided that it was a career that I want to pursue.
